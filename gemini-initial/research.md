2. Competitor Analysis
Competitor 1: Traditional Agencies

    Strength: High touch service and deep manual analysis.
    Weakness: Slow execution cycles and high monthly retainers.
    Your Advantage: You offer a rapid, AI-driven framework that iterates in days.

Competitor 2: SaaS Analytics Platforms

    Strength: Automated data visualization and real-time dashboards.
    Weakness: They provide the "what" but fail to provide the "how" for strategy.
    Your Advantage: You close the loop between data and actionable marketing messages.
